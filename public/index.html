Welcome to the Coptic Calendar

We wanted to have a condensed place where we could find today's readings of the day, saints of the day, and fasts and feasts of the day. This project is an effort at bringing you an easy to read tool that condenses all the church history into one!

Here are the different ways you can access the tool:

  • Scriptable Widget for iPhone
  • Website View (Mobile Compatible)
  • Chrome, Firefox, and Brave Extension for new tabs
